Indian Butter Chicken

deliciously intense

<-- Back

Chicken Makhani, also known as Indian Butter Chicken, is a rich and flavorful dish that combines tender chicken pieces with a creamy tomato-based sauce. Infused with aromatic spices, ginger garlic paste, and a hint of lemon, this recipe delivers a delightful balance of heat and sweetness. Perfect for a cozy dinner, serve this hearty dish over basmati rice or with warm naan bread to soak up the luxurious sauce.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons peanut oil, divided
  • 1 shallot, finely chopped
  • ¼ white onion, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon ginger garlic paste
  • 2 teaspoons lemon juice
  • 2 teaspoons garam masala, divided
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1 cup half-and-half
  • ¼ cup plain yogurt
  • 1 pinch salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-size pieces
  • ¼ teaspoon cayenne pepper, or to taste
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • ¼ cup water

Directions

  1. Heat 1 tablespoon peanut oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Cook and stir shallot and onion until tender, about 5 minutes. Reduce heat to low.
  2. Melt butter in the same skillet. Stir in ginger garlic paste, lemon juice, 1 teaspoon garam masala, chili powder, cumin, and bay leaf. Cook, stirring frequently, until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  3. Stir in tomato puree and cook for 2 minutes, stirring frequently. Add half-and-half and yogurt; cook and stir until sauce thickens, about 3 minutes. Season with salt and black pepper. Remove from heat and set aside.
  4. Heat 1 tablespoon peanut oil in a large heavy skillet over medium heat. Cook chicken until lightly browned, about 10 minutes; reduce heat to low. Season with remaining 1 teaspoon garam masala and cayenne. Stir in cornstarch and water; cook until sauce thickens and chicken is no longer pink, about 5 minutes.
  5. Transfer cooked chicken to sauce. Simmer until flavors meld, about 5 minutes.
  6. Remove and discard bay leaf. Garnish with cilantro and serve.
